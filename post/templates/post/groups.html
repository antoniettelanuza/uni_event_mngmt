Organizational And Departmental Groups

BSIT-3 GROUP

The BSIT-3 Group is a dynamic community of students focused on exploring the latest trends and technologies in the field of Information Technology. This group serves as a collaborative platform for discussing various IT-related topics, from programming languages to network security, and providing support for academic projects. Together, we aim to enhance our technical skills, share knowledge, and prepare for real-world challenges in the tech industry.

SPUSurigao - Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants

The Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants (JPIA) is a prestigious organization dedicated to the development and enhancement of future accountants. Through collaboration, learning, and various events, the group prepares its members for successful careers in the field of accounting and finance.
